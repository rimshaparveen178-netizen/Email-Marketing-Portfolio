## About

I'm Rimsha Parveen, Director of Outbound Marketing at OpiMax Incorporated. This portfolio is a selection of the email and SMS marketing work I'm proudest of — built across seven ecommerce brands in beauty, home goods, sporting goods, and food & beverage.

The portfolio covers:

- **Multi-brand management** — running seven Klaviyo accounts simultaneously across four verticals
- **Master Spa Parts case study** — a full lifecycle program with 14+ flows, 30+ segments, 12+ product feeds, custom analytics, and SMS/RCS messaging
- **Shopper Approved integration** — 62,682 verified reviews powering email content
- **The Working Cycle** — my repeatable six-phase methodology for onboarding and running a new client account, from access acquisition to scheduled live campaigns
